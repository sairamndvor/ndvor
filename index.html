ndVOR is a technology services company founded in 2009, specializing in delivering innovative IT solutions that drive business transformation. Our name phonetically sounds like "Endeavor," reflecting our commitment to striving for excellence in everything we do.

Jaikishen K
President
Jai founded ndVOR with a vision to provide exceptional services to customers by leveraging technology to solve business problems. An accomplished marketing professional with a foundation of management excellence, Jai brings a hands-on approach to management with rich insights to organizational effectiveness, business development, and strategic planning.
In a career spanning over two decades, Jai has had vast exposure to International Sales, Product Marketing and Service delivery in the Information Technology and Telecom industries. He has led the launch of industry-leading products for the Research Community in the Higher Education and Medical Sectors in the U.S.
Jai has successfully grown an IBM technologies services business which culminated in a merger with MindTree Consulting, where he took on the role of managing the combined IBM services business for North America and Europe. He has held country responsibilities for Motorola's customer-owned messaging business and excelled at various sales and product marketing roles for Digital Equipment Corporation.
Jai has a Master's in Marketing Management from the University of Bombay. He is based out of New Jersey, USA.
Manoj Vellakkat
Head – New Business & Operations
Manoj brings over two decades of experience in delivery, practice building, pre-sales and managing customer relationships. As Head – New Business & Operations, Manoj is responsible for profitable business growth at ndVOR. He is also responsible for building a passionate set of professionals with a “Can do” attitude who are sensitive to customer requirements. Manoj also takes care of the IT and Operations function of the organization.
